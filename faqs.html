Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About Selling Structured Settlement Payments

1. How quickly can I get a quote for my structured settlement?

We provide instant, upfront quotes online using our industry-first calculator. You'll know the estimated value of your structured settlement payments immediately based on the information you provide.

2. What information do I need to get an estimate?

You simply need details about your structured settlement payments, including the amount, payment frequency, and whether payments are life-contingent or guaranteed.

3. Is my upfront estimate guaranteed?

Your upfront estimate is highly accurate based on the provided information. Final quotes may vary slightly after we confirm details during the verification process.

4. What documents are required to sell my structured settlement?

Typically, we'll need your structured settlement agreement, release agreement, and identification documents. Our team assists you through the entire paperwork process.

5. How long does the selling process typically take?

The selling process usually takes between 45 and 90 days, depending on state requirements, court schedules, and the specifics of your structured settlement.

6. Do I have to sell all of my structured settlement payments?

No, you can choose to sell either all or a portion of your future payments. We provide personalized options tailored to your financial goals.

7. How does court approval work?

Selling structured settlement payments requires court approval to ensure your best interests are protected. We handle all legal proceedings, making the process straightforward and hassle-free for you.

8. What is a life-contingent structured settlement payment?

Life-contingent payments are structured settlement payments that depend on your lifespan. They aren't guaranteed if the original recipient passes away, unlike guaranteed payments.

9. How does SmarterPayouts protect my family with life-contingent payments?

We offer a unique family protection feature—essentially a shared death benefit—for life-contingent payments. This ensures your loved ones receive financial protection, providing peace of mind in the event of your passing.

10. Are there any hidden fees involved?

We believe in complete transparency—there are no hidden fees or unexpected charges. All fees are clearly outlined and communicated upfront.

11. Why should I choose SmarterPayouts to sell my structured settlement?

SmarterPayouts offers unmatched transparency with instant, upfront estimates, personalized support, innovative tools, and industry-leading family protection features. We’re dedicated to ensuring you have complete clarity and comfort throughout the selling process.

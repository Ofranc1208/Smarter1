Frequently Asked Questions

What is a structured settlement?

A structured settlement is a financial arrangement typically resulting from a lawsuit, where periodic payments are made over time.

Can I sell only a part of my settlement?

Yes. We offer flexible options that allow you to sell all or just part of your structured settlement payments.

How quickly can I get my payout?

The timeframe typically ranges from 45-90 days, depending on court approval and required documentation.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. SmarterPayouts prides itself on full transparency. The amount quoted is what you'll receive.

Our Methodology: The SmarterPayouts Difference

SmarterPayouts redefines the structured settlement industry by offering a transparent, efficient, and customer-first approach. Our proven methodology ensures you get the most value from your structured settlement payments without stress, uncertainty, or hidden fees.

Step 1: Instant, Upfront Pricing – No Guesswork

  • Use our advanced online calculator to receive an instant estimate of your structured settlement's value.
  • Review your quote with full transparency—no lengthy consultations required.
  • If you decide to proceed, receive a personalized offer based on your payment schedule.
  • Electronic disclosure documents are sent via DocuSign for your acknowledgment.

Step 2: Offer Disclosure & Notarized Signing

  • Receive an official disclosure package detailing your early payout offer, start/end dates, and payment breakdowns.
  • A licensed notary is arranged to oversee the signing process at your preferred location.
  • If required, additional signatures (e.g., from a spouse) are collected.
  • The notary verifies your identity and returns the completed documents for processing.

Step 3: Legal Review & Court Date Scheduling

  • Our legal team reviews all paperwork for accuracy and compliance with state regulations.
  • Documents are submitted to our attorney who files the necessary paperwork with the court.
  • A court hearing date is scheduled, typically within 15 to 25 days.
  • Our attorney coordinates with the court to ensure a smooth approval process.

Step 4: Court Approval & Fund Disbursement

  • A local district judge reviews your transfer request to ensure it’s in your best interest.
  • If approved, all relevant parties (including the issuing insurance company) are notified.
  • The funding process is initiated immediately, with options for bank wire transfer or physical check.
  • You gain full control over your lump sum payment once funds are disbursed.

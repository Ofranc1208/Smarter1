How Our Process Works

At SmarterPayouts, we understand that selling your structured settlement payments is a significant financial decision. Our goal is to provide a fast, transparent, and hassle-free process so you can access your money when you need it most. With our industry-first online calculator, you receive an upfront estimate without any obligations. Here’s how our streamlined process works:

Step 1: Get Your Free Quote & Accept the Offer

  • Use our online calculator to get an instant estimate of your structured settlement’s value.
  • Review your quote at your convenience—there’s no obligation.
  • If you decide to proceed, our team will present you with a personalized offer based on your payment schedule.
  • Once you accept, we send the required offer disclosure documents via DocuSign for electronic acknowledgment.
  • Most states require a review period before signing the official transfer agreement to ensure you understand the terms.

Step 2: Offer Disclosure & Notarized Signing

  • After accepting the offer, we send you an official disclosure package, including:
    • Your early payout offer
    • Your start and end dates
    • Detailed payment breakdowns
  • A licensed notary is arranged to meet with you at your preferred location for signing.
  • If required by law, your spouse or another co-signer may also need to sign.
  • The notary verifies your identity, oversees the signing process, and returns the completed documents to our office for processing.

Step 3: Legal Review & Court Date Scheduling

  • After the transfer agreement is signed, our legal team reviews all paperwork for accuracy and compliance with state regulations.
  • The documents are submitted to our attorney, who files the necessary paperwork with the court.
  • A court hearing date is scheduled, typically within 15 to 25 days from submission.
  • Our attorney handles all legal filings and coordinates with the court to ensure a smooth process.

Step 4: Court Approval & Fund Disbursement

  • A local district judge reviews your transfer request to ensure it’s in your best interest.
  • If approved, the court notifies all relevant parties—including the insurance company that issued your structured settlement.
  • The funding process is initiated immediately, and you receive your funds through your preferred method:
    • Bank wire transfer (fastest option)
    • Physical check (if preferred)
  • Once the funds are disbursed, you have full control over how to use your lump sum payment.

Understanding the Court Approval Process

Selling structured settlement payments requires court approval—an essential step to protect your financial interests. At SmarterPayouts, we guide you through this process to ensure everything is handled smoothly and efficiently. A local judge reviews the transaction to ensure it meets legal requirements and serves your best interest.

Why Does Court Approval Matter?

  • Protect the Seller: The judge ensures that selling your structured settlement will not leave you in financial hardship.
  • Verify Fairness: The court confirms that you understand the transaction and that the offer is reasonable.
  • Ensure Compliance: Legal requirements vary by state, and court approval ensures that everything is done correctly.
  • Provide Final Authorization: Once approved, the sale is legally binding, and funds can be disbursed.

What Happens During Court Approval?

Once your Transfer Agreement is signed and notarized, the following steps occur:

  1. Legal Submission: Our legal team prepares and files the required paperwork with the court.
  2. Court Hearing Scheduled: The court assigns a hearing date, typically 15-25 days after submission.
  3. Judge’s Review: During the hearing, a judge reviews the case to ensure the sale is in your best interest.
  4. Approval Granted: If approved, we receive the final court order to proceed.
  5. Funds Disbursed: Once court approval is secured, we initiate the payout process.

Why Judges View Our Family Protection Plan Favorably

One major concern with selling life-contingent payments is that if the annuitant passes away, future payments stop entirely. Our Family Protection Plan is designed to address this concern:

  • What is the Family Protection Plan? – With this plan, you not only receive your money upfront but also ensure your family is protected in unforeseen circumstances.
  • Why Do Courts Favor This? – Judges are more likely to approve transactions where the seller’s family remains financially secure, ensuring that even if future payments cease, your loved ones receive the full value.

Why Selling Future Payments Can Be a Better Option

  • ✅ Immediate Access to Cash: Instead of waiting years for periodic payments, you receive a lump sum today.
  • ✅ Flexibility & Control: Use your funds for investments, debt relief, or major purchases.
  • ✅ Protection Against Inflation: Receiving your payout now maximizes your purchasing power.
  • ✅ Avoid Uncertainty: If your annuity is life-contingent, selling ensures you receive the full amount upfront.

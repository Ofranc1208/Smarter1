At SmarterPayouts, we believe in complete transparency, ensuring that you fully understand every step of the structured settlement payout process. The signing process consists of two key documents before final approval: the Disclosure Agreement and the Transfer Agreement. Once these are completed, a notary will oversee the final signing to ensure everything is legally binding.

Step 2: Offer Disclosure & Notarized Signing

  • After accepting the offer, you will receive an official disclosure package, which includes:
    • Your early payout offer

  • A licensed notary will be arranged to meet with you at your preferred location for signing.

Step 3: Legal Review & Court Date Scheduling

Once the transfer agreement is signed, our legal team reviews all paperwork to ensure accuracy and compliance with state regulations. The documents are then submitted to our attorney, who files the necessary paperwork with the court. A hearing date is scheduled, typically within 15 to 25 days, and our attorney coordinates with the court for a smooth process.
